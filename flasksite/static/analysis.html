Segment Advice
Are there locations in NYC where the "Fina a Taxi" walking direction advice is especially beneficial? Analytically this can be phrased as: Are there streets where the differential in taxi frequency at each end is very large? This analysis map explores the benefit of the walking direction advice by comparing the taxi pickup frequency at each end of road segments.
Dropoff/Pickup Imbalance
Are there areas in the city where the difference between pickups and dropoffs at an intersection is very large and persistent. This analysis examines places where it may be especially easy or hard to find a taxi based on the relative frequencies of pickups and dropoffs.
