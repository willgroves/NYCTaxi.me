Find a Taxi
Please click on the "Find a Taxi" button above to get information about the best direction to walk from your current location. Recommendations are generated by analyzing over 160 million taxi fare records from the New York City Taxi & Limousine Commission.
Analysis
The analysis section uncovers insights about the direction recommendation construced by the "Find a Taxi" function. Regions where the direction recommendations are most informative are shown. Also, the difference between pickup and drop off rates for frequently visited intersections are examined in detail. Additional information about how these recommendations are generated is also provided.
